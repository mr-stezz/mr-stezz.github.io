Apr 2014—Oct 2014

Wonder PL for web

I designed the web application, internally known as Romeo, for uploading videos and collaborating on projects with stakeholders. It allowed film-makers and production houses to comment on cuts of a video and then re-upload assets and re-cuts for further assessment. We interviewed a few professional indie film-makers to test our hypotheses and if the product was viable. Built as a single page app in Angular, I worked with developers Johan Goransson and Phil Shum to refine the flows and visual design of the app.

I worked briefly with Fabio Basile and Ryan Murphy to update the visual language on the Wonder PL iOS app. I updated its icon and assets, supporting the new iPhone 6 plus. We also started working on a feature we called Shop Motion, which allowed users to purchase goods that were featured in a video. Developed by Cong Le and Sherman Lo.
