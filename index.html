Apr 2014—Oct 2014

Wonder PL for web

I designed the web application, internally known as Romeo, for uploading videos and collaborating on projects with stakeholders. It allowed film-makers and production houses to comment on cuts of a video and then re-upload assets and re-cuts for further assessment. We interviewed a few professional indie film-makers to test our hypotheses and if the product was viable. Built as a single page app in Angular, I worked with developers Johan Goransson and Phil Shum to refine the flows and visual design of the app. Unfortunately, the app is no longer in development.

We also started working on a feature we called Shop Motion, which allowed users to purchase goods that were featured in a video. Developed by Cong Le and Sherman Lo. Unfortunately, the app is no longer in development.
