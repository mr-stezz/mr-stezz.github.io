OnCare

2016—2017 with Alistair Cohen

OnCare is a B2B SaaS business from the Founders Factory incubator. OnCare provides tools that make caring for the elderly easier. The business has three apps which I helped prototype and design. This includes a React Native app for both iOS and Android and a responsive web app. I worked on product strategy, prototypes and informing the user experience, as well as the creative direction.
