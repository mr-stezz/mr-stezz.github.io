Culture Trip

2017—present

Culture Trip is a media, entertainment and technology company in the travel vertical. I joined as a product designer to work alongside the small product team on improving the website and native mobile apps. In early 2018, the company raised 80 million dollars in a series B round, continuing its mission to shine a light on different cultures worldwide. I focused on improving conversions for an affiliate booking program. After seeing improvements, the company decided to enter the OTA space, I designed the first round of the booking experience. Since then, I now continue to design experiments around the site to drive traffic.
