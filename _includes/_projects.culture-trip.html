2017—Present

Culture Trip is a content, e-commerce and technology company in the travel vertical.

I joined as a product designer to work alongside the small product and tech team, based in Tel Aviv, on improving the website and native mobile apps. In early 2018, the company raised 80 million dollars in a series B round, continuing its mission to shine a light on different cultures worldwide. I focused on improving conversions for an affiliate booking program.

After seeing improvements, the company decided to enter the OTA space, I designed the first round of the booking experience.

After that, I focused on experiments that drove traffic around the site directing our readers to conversion oportunities.

Continuing on, I worked within the content and community squad, responsible for designing content production experiences for our freelancers.

I am now working on improving the booking experiences for both of our native mobile apps which you can download from the App Store and Play Store, respectively.
