Culture Trip

2017—Present

Culture Trip is a media, entertainment and technology company in the travel vertical.

I joined as a product designer to work alongside the small product team on improving the website and native mobile apps. In early 2018, the company raised 80 million dollars in a series B round, continuing its mission to shine a light on different cultures worldwide. I focused on improving conversions for an affiliate booking program.

After seeing improvements, the company decided to enter the OTA space, I designed the first round of the booking experience.

After that, I focused on experiments that drove traffic around the site directing them to conversion oportunities. I now work within the content operations team, responsible for designing content production experiences.
