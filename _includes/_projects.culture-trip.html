Culture Trip #

2017—Present

Culture Trip is a content, e-commerce and technology company in the travel vertical.

Affiliate Bookings #

I joined as a product designer to work alongside the small product and tech team, based in Tel Aviv, on improving the website and native mobile apps. In early 2018, the company raised 80 million dollars in a series B round, continuing its mission to shine a light on different cultures worldwide. I focused on improving conversions for an affiliate booking program.

Online Travel Agent #

After seeing some initial positive signals, the company decided to enter the OTA space, I designed the first round of the booking experience.

Growth/Pathways To Purchase #

After that, I focused on experiments that drove traffic around the site directing our readers to conversion opportunities.

Content & Community #

Continuing on, I worked within the content and community squad, responsible for designing content production experiences for our freelancers in the form of our very own 'Creator App' web app.

Bookable Experiences #

I worked on improving booking related experiences across our website and both of our native mobile apps which you can download from the App Store and Play Store, respectively.

Native Checkout for Apps #

Building off the amazing work that had been done on the Checkout via the web, I designed all the native checkout experiences for the app and tried to get the experience feeling like a first party citizen on the respective platforms.

Apple Pay for Web & Apps #

I designed how Apple Pay would work via the web checkout and mobile apps natively. I also sat in with the developers and offered a technical solution to implement the Apple Pay button.
